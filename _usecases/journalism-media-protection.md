Source protection, secure communications, and censorship resistance for journalists, news organizations, and media outlets.

Journalism requires secure communications to protect sources, maintain editorial independence, and resist censorship. Traditional platforms expose journalists and their sources to surveillance, censorship, and retaliation that can compromise press freedom.

#### Key Benefits
- **Source Protection**: Secure communications that protect journalist sources
- **Censorship Resistance**: Communications that work even under censorship attempts
- **Editorial Security**: Protected newsroom communications and story development
- **Anonymous Communications**: No personal identifiers required for sensitive reporting

#### Perfect for:
- Investigative journalism
- Source communication
- Newsroom coordination
- International reporting
- Sensitive story development

#### Why Choose Diode for Journalism
Diode provides the security and censorship resistance journalists need to protect sources and maintain press freedom. With no server storage and anonymous communications, journalists can report freely while protecting their sources and editorial independence.
