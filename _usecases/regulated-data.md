Diode Collab is a great way to keep your current regulated environments intact, while providing remote access to your suppliers and extended team.

Handling regulated data like PII, HIPAA-protected information, or other sensitive data requires strict security controls and compliance measures. Traditional cloud solutions often create compliance challenges by storing data on third-party servers.

#### Key Benefits
- **Compliance Ready**: Designed for PII, HIPAA, and other regulatory requirements
- **No Cloud Storage**: Data never leaves your controlled environment
- **Audit Trail**: Complete logging and monitoring for compliance reporting
- **Secure Collaboration**: Enable team access without compromising security

#### Perfect for:
- Healthcare organizations
- Financial services
- Legal firms
- Government agencies
- Any organization handling regulated data
