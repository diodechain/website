Establish full custody of your organization's data and communications and eliminate data, identity, and behavioral leakages that could unintentionally violate regional data restrictions.

#### Remove points of vulnerability
Give your most vulnerable people the tools they need to operate securely in their persons, data, and communications.

Global teams face unique security challenges, especially when operating across different regulatory environments. Traditional collaboration tools often create data sovereignty issues and expose teams to various security risks.

#### Key Benefits
- **Full Data Custody**: Complete control over organizational data and communications
- **Regional Compliance**: Automatically meet local data protection requirements
- **Secure Communications**: Protect team members in sensitive locations
- **No Data Leakage**: Eliminate identity and behavioral data exposure
