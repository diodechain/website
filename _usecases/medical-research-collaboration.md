- **Regulatory Compliance**: Meets FDA and research institution requirements
- **Multi-Site Collaboration**: Secure coordination across research locations
- **Patient Privacy**: Protected participant information and study data

- Clinical trial coordination
- Multi-site research studies
- Research data sharing
- Regulatory submission preparation
- Academic medical collaboration
