Subscribing to Diode Collab's Team plan

  1. Subscribe to a single user (a quantity of 1)
  2. You will receive a receipt code from Stripe
  3. Apply the receipt code to the Zone you are upgrading
  4. Your subscription will automatically update to reflect the actual number of users in your Zone

Watch a video to see how this works.
