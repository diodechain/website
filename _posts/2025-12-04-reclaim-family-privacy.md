### The Problem

Every photo, message, and document shared usually passes through, and often stays on, someone else’s server. Even “secure” messaging apps rely on centralized systems that can be logged, exposed, or linked to personal identifiers like phone numbers and emails.

If you’ve ever wondered whether there’s a safer way for families to communicate online, you’re not alone.

### The Solution

Diode Collab™ lets families communicate and share without servers, clouds, or data collection. Unlike apps like Signal or WhatsApp, all messages and media stay on your own devices. They’re never stored in anyone else’s hands.

### Simple, Unlimited, and Private

Families can stay organized without sacrificing privacy. Create group chats, swap schedules, and store photos or important documents: all with unlimited space. There’s no need to hand over phone numbers or personal details to get started, and every interaction inside your Zones stays encrypted and secure.

### Only Stores on Your Devices

Diode Collab™ connects your family’s devices directly, so nothing ever passes through or lives on a cloud server. No one outside your household can scan, store, or monetize your conversations or files. Your data stays local, under your control, with no central point of failure or exposure.

Decentralized by design, Diode Collab™ means no outside party ever touches your family’s data. 

### A New Standard for Privacy

Most messaging apps like still tie your identity to a phone number, route traffic through centralized servers, or keep copies of your data in places you can’t see. Diode removes those risks entirely. 

Your family can connect without exposing contact details, and every conversation, photo, and document stays encrypted and stored only on your own devices: not in someone else’s infrastructure. It’s privacy that doesn’t depend on trust in a company, only on the devices you control.
